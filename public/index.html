Things to consider when buying a new pair of skates

A hobby I picked up this past year is rollerblading. Rollerblading is quite fun and can be great exercise. Choosing the right pair of skates is very important in getting the most our of your skating experience.

Choosing the right pair of skates is paramount. There is some homework to do before you get your first pair. One key aspect of finding the right pair of skates is deciding between four wheels and three.

A four wheel setup will give you more stability but less agility. A four wheel setup will also help in acceleration more so than a three wheel setup because you have on extra point of contact with the ground to get going. A three wheel setup will have less stability but more agility because with three wheels you will have a shorter frame. As previously mentioned, a four wheel setup will have better acceleration, but a three wheel setup will maintain top speed more efficiently since there is one less contact point to the ground to slow you down.

Number of wheels is just one decision you have to make when buying the right pair of skates. Frame size/height, shell material, and wheel size are other things to consider when purchasing a pair of skates. If you give it a go and carefully decide on the right pair, tons of fun awaits!
